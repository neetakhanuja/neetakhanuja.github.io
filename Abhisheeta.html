Abhisheeta, a story of human desires.

An academic project at National Institute Of Design, India.
An experimental project exploring various aspects of performance arts.
Intention of this project was to create an immersive experience for viewers using a collage format, with projected graphics, text, performers and sound as its elements.
It was an exploration in storytelling, depicted how the chase of desires never end.
Performance was choreographed and shot at DanceOceans, dance studio in Pune.
